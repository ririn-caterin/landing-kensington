Template Features!

Meet our awesome features of Kensington property comes with. These are very powerful for you to build booking or property website.

Customizable Advanced Property Search

Help your visitors find the right property by letting them set various search criteria such as size and price range. Customize your search easily with a flexible back-end settings panel

Booking Module

Easily transform your website into a Booking Platform and enable several features such as availability calendar, invoice system, payment integration, and many more.

Google Maps Integration

It’s very easy to find your dream properties on Google Maps with Kensington property. Find property by location, type, status, keyword, number of bed, and other search field that you can customize. We also complete google maps with customizable marker icon and map style

Compare Properties

Let your users compare up to 4 different properties based on their features and parameters

Page Builder Integrated

Create unlimited homepage variation by our awesome page builder. Drag and drop homepage elements without changing codes with many available blocks. It comes with 6 pre-set for quick set-up. It is easy and fast!

Built-in Booking Module

Transform your website into a Booking Platform and enable these features

  • Availability Calendar
  • Booking Management
  • Invoicing System
  • Paypal Integration
  • Advanced Search with available dates and guests
  • Changeable check-in hours
  • Custom price per day
  • Manually set unbookable dates
