Customizable Advanced Property Search

Booking Module

Easily transform your website into a Kensington Booking Platform and enable several features such as availability calendar, invoice system, payment integration, and many more.

Google Maps Integration

It’s very easy to find your dream properties on Google Maps with Kensington Property. Find property by location, type, status, keyword, number of bed, and other search field that you can customize. We also complete google maps with customizable marker icon and map style.
